About LiveMemories

LiveMemories is a smart journaling app that lets you capture, store, and analyze your most precious memories using cutting-edge technology. With cloud syncing, AI-powered reminders, and an elegant design, you’ll never lose a meaningful moment again.

Whether you want to record a video memory, upload it to the cloud, or receive daily prompts to remember, LiveMemories is built for your digital life.
